Spotify

 

 A newcomer to the audiobook arena, Spotify, the music streaming platform is now 

offering a paid audiobook service that is separate from it’s massive music and 

podcast catalogue. The audiobook content is available on pay-per-item basis, 

so you only pay for the audiobooks you want so there’s no monthly or annual fee. 

Content currently has to be purchased in the Spotify Web Player, but can be 

listened to through the app.
